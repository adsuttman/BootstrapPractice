A SuperNETural Phenomenon is an occurence of the Other Side seeping into our world.

SNP's usually result in the appearance of strange websites, or in stronger cases, modifications of existing ones.

The Other Side is a term used to refer to a parallel dimension to ours.

Not much is known about the Other Side as we cannot perceive it from our dimension.

At their weakest, SNP's merely cause the existence of strange webpages. But, the more their pages are viewed the more power seeps from the Other Side.

If sn SNP gains enough power, it can result in an Real Space Crossover which can be very dangerous.

As such, you should avoid even the harmless SNP's.

A Real Space Crossover is a temporary merging of our dimension and the Other Side, that can allow residents from the Other Side to affect our world more directly than an SNP.

These events are often extremely dangerous.

It is not known RSC can cause something from our world to go to the Other Side, although it is theoretically possible.

If it has happened, no one has lived to tell the tale.

SuperNETural Tourists are people who like to find and visit SNP's for fun.

We do not recommend this practice as it can be very dangerous and make the SNP's stronger.
